## brief bio
Chi Hui is a prolific short-fiction writer and longtime editor at *Science Fiction World* (科幻世界) in Chengdu — the dominant short-SF publication in mainland China. Her fiction tends toward biological and AI speculation with a lyrical register. She is less internationally visible than Hao Jingfang or Xia Jia, largely because her novel-length work has not yet been translated, but within the Chinese SF community she is a foundational editorial and authorial figure. English-language biographical sourcing for Chi Hui is sparse — this is a genuine gap, and readers should treat bio details as approximate.
