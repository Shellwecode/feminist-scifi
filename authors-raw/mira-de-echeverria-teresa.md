# Teresa P. Mira de Echeverría

## name
Teresa P. Mira de Echeverría

## primary writing language
Spanish

## country / region
Argentina (Latin American)

## years active
2000s–present

## monumental works
- **Memory** (2014)
    - form: novel
    - Speculative / historical-fantastic; one of her best-known Spanish-language SF novels.
- **"Other Shores" / "Umbría"** (various short stories)
    - form: short stories
    - Published in *Próxima*, *Axxón*, and other Argentine SF venues.
- **"Like the Others"** (English tr. "Como los otros" in anthologies)
    - form: short story
    - Anthologised in English-language Latin American SF collections.

## brief bio
Teresa P. Mira de Echeverría is an Argentine writer and philosophy-of-science scholar whose short SF has circulated through the Argentine and Spanish genre small-press ecosystem (*Axxón*, *Próxima*) since the mid-2000s. Her work pushes against the literary-fantastic default of Latin American speculative writing, leaning toward hard-SF concerns about time, cognition, and language. Anglophone visibility depends heavily on anthology appearances; English-language biographical detail is thinner than for her more commercially translated contemporaries. Included here as a representative of the working-writer stratum of contemporary Latin American SF, not only the Booker-tier breakout names.
